Moroccan Chicken Salad

This salad is light and without much calories. It is perfect for those looking to enjoy a healthy meal that still tastes great without worrying about ruining their diet. Its main ingredients are rice, chicken and lettuce. It costs €5.65.

Falafel

Falafel is a deep fried ball or patty made with ground chickpeas or fav beans, spices & herbs. It contains healthy fats which are good for you and doesn't raise cholesterol like trans fats and saturated fats. It originates from the middle-east, it contains spiced mashed chickpeas formed into balls or fritters and deep-fried. It is served in a spinach wrap and comes with hummus, shredded courgette, cucumber, red onion and carrot with mango chutney. It costs €6.60.
