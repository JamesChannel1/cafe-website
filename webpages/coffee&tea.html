Americano

An Americano coffee is a shot of espresso, topped up with hot water. An espresso shot is created by forcing pressurized, near boiling water through very tightly packed and finely ground beans. The high pressure extracts the oils from the coffee beans into the shot and also enables a quicker brewing process. Americano is priced at €2.85 and is nice with biscuits and chocolate.

Latte

The latté is a very popular coffee which is made up of a shot of espresso coffee and steamed milk. This results in a creamy smooth coffee beverage with a delicate espresso flavor that has a layer of milk foam on top. It is served hot and originates from Italy. It is priced at €3.25.

Mocha

A mocha is similar to a latte but is made of a shot of espresso, then chocolate powder or syrup, followed by milk or cream. The drink is named after the chocolate tasting mocha beans which were shipped from the Al Mokka port in Yemen. The mocha doesn't contain mocha beans but it's still called this because of its chocolate flavour. It is priced at €3.45.

Espresso

Espressos are strong, small black coffees made by forcing steam through ground coffee beans. It originates from Italy and is the earliest form of coffee. Depending on one or two shots it is priced at €2.05 or €2.55.
