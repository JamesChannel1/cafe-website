Café owner Bob

Bob is the new owner of Limetree café since 2019. He bought the café from the previous owners and is passionate about coffee and running a successful small business. Additionally, limetree café is now affiliated with Dublin tourism. The mission is now to manage a business that is always improving in it's service to customers and tourists which meets their needs and adds value.

Bob is a dynamic self-starter that has created a business before. In 2017 he opened a restaurant business and bar which unfortunately ended up failing. However, this has given Bob more experience, which will ultimately contribute to helping Limetree succeed.

Staff member Sarah

A recent hire, Sarah has experience in service and food catering. She joined limetree café as a waitress and is a helpful and friendly person. Previously, Sarah has worked at starbucks also as a waitress.

Sarah is passionate about veganism and animal rights. She likes to help out at the animal shelter and owns multiple pet cats.
