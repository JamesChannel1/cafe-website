Cookies

Our cookies at limetree cafe contain raisins and priced at €2.50. They are crunchy and tasty, going well with any warm beverage whether tea, coffee or hot chocolate.

Croissant

Croissants are buttery and flakey pastries. They originate from Austria and France, being named after their crescent shape. They are priced at €2.75 or €2.85 for an almond croissant.

Caramel squares

Caramel squares are a baked good made of caramalised sugar and chocolate. They are tasty and go well with hot chocolate and coffee. They are priced From €3.25.

Sausage rolls

Sausage rolls are a popular pastry which consists of sausage wrapped in puffed pastry. They are common in the UK, ireland and other countries such as Canada and Australia. At limetree they are priced at €2.85.
