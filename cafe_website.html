Welcome

Our café has great coffees, snacks and is conveniently located beside kilmainham gaol prison museum. It is a nice place to relax and enjoy a warm beverage as well as a suitable venue for meeting with friends and family for talking and spending time. There is free wifi and friendly servers who are happy to help. Also, we serve a lunch menu with salads, sandwiches and soups. There is a sandwich of the day and soup of the day, which can be pleasant and warming during winter time.

Opening Times:

Mon - Sat: 9am to 4.30pm

Sun: 10am to 4.30pm
